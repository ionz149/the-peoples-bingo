About this game

This game is loosely based on bingo and was built as a means to better understand Javascript. My wife and I love watching People's Court re-runs and keep a running list of our favorite quotes so this was a simple and fun way two merge to things I enjoy. The general vibe is to fire up youtube, pull up The People's Court, and watch a few episodes to try and get a bingo. In it's current iteration it would take quite a few episodes to get a bingo.

Congratulations!

If you made it this far you must really like The People's Court.
